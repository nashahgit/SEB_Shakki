Services

Build a Plan

        Through our ‘build a plan’ service, we help to prepare academic and educational plans for setting up institutes like schools, nurseries, training institutes. With the market knowledge and experience, largely our focus area is Middle East and India. Our expertise also branches into preparing market study & marketing plans, product development, feasibility studies related to educational sectors etc.

Operate an Institution

        We can take over struggling educational institutions. Make a detailed study and suggest improvement plans to increase profitability. Our expert team can also help in marketing such institutes with our in-house capabilities. Such marketing initiatives and takeovers are done with the support of existing management / organizational setups in those institutes. Our team can also independently run struggling institutes and hand over once into the profit making mode.

Knowledge Bridging

        We act as a bridge between various professional bodies across the globe and educational institutions in the Middle East and India. Our organization will be the single window to enroll for programs conducted by these professional bodies. Being regional representatives for various professional institutions, we ensure the best service is given including conducting exams, classes, and other activities as specified by such professional bodies.

Educational Consultancy

        Through our consultancy cell, we guide aspirants and students on educational institutions, admission procedures and other related queries for pursuing studies outside their country. This service is available for institutes based in Middle East, United Kingdom, United States of America, Australia and India.

        Being coordinators for professional bodies and foreign universities we can undertake university affiliation services too. With these services we help educational institutions to get affiliations from universities based in USA, UK, Australia or India.

Seminars and Conferences

        We thoroughly understand that education in modern days have changed from reading text books or writing notes to more interactive ways of practical illustrations and case studies. In light of such changes we felt the need to having experts in chosen fields who can interact with students with real world issues on subjects they pursue. We are capable of conducting various seminars and conferences – both in house and external covering Accounting, Finance, Management, Hospitality and Logistics. Though only subject knowledge won’t suffice the need to the changing business world today, we also conduct programs in developing soft skills like Leadership and Personality Development as well.
